subsidystories.eu

Subsidystories.eu intends to increase transparency of EU-Funds by unravelling how the European Structural Investment Funds are spent at the local level. We publish here all the beneficiary data available in different portals everywhere in one place: search and analyse!

Explore the available data. Click on the map, choose from the dropdown list or the country list on the right-hand side.
